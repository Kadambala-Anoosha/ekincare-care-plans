What your report may have flagged

Three silent risks. One smart move each.

High Blood Pressure

The risk: Silently scars your heart, kidneys, and brain. 2× stroke risk. India's #1 cause of kidney failure.

Best move: Track BP daily, cut sodium, walk 30 min, manage stress & sleep.

Pre-Diabetes & Diabetes

The risk: 70% of pre-diabetics turn Type 2 within 8 years. Damages eyes, nerves, kidneys — often before symptoms.

Best move: See your sugar live with a CGM, fix glycemic load, add strength training.

The risk: Belly fat drives BP up, dulls insulin, and inflames arteries — the root behind both risks above.

Best move: Drop 5–7% body weight via a macro-balanced plan and habit coaching — most metabolic markers reset.
